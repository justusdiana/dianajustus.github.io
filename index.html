About Me

Hello, and welcome to my very first website! My name is Diana Justus. A little bit about me, I am originally from Peru. I have been living in Michigan for over 13 years now. I was 18 years old when I first came to the United States with basic English, alone, but full of dreams. Getting used to a new country, a different language, climate, food, and culture was a big challenge, but I was determined to overcome every single challenge that came my way. I'm a proud mother of two beautiful and super smart kids, and a wife to a wonderful husband. Now that our kids are full time students, I have decided to go back to college and pursuit my associate's degree in CIS. Technology has always been a path that I was interested in. Back in my country I was studying Software Engineering but the opportunity to come to the United States presented itself and I took a leap of faith, and here I am, trying to make the best of what this life has to offer everyday. This is my first attempt to create a website. I hope you enjoy my very simple yet very well loved website.
